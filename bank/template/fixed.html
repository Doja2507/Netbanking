Fixed Deposit Information

Looking for a secure investment option with guaranteed returns? Consider opening a fixed deposit account with us. Fixed deposits, also known as term deposits, offer a safe and reliable way to grow your savings over time.

With a fixed deposit account, you can deposit a lump sum amount for a specified period, ranging from a few months to several years. During this period, your deposit earns a fixed interest rate, which is higher than regular savings accounts.

Fixed deposits are an ideal choice for individuals who want to earn steady returns on their savings without taking on any risk. Whether you're planning for a major purchase, building an emergency fund, or saving for retirement, fixed deposits can help you achieve your financial goals.

Opening a fixed deposit account is simple and straightforward. If you're an existing customer, you can log in to your account and create a fixed deposit online. If you're new to our bank, you can easily apply for an account through our website and then proceed to create a fixed deposit.

Our fixed deposit accounts come with flexible terms, competitive interest rates, and the option to choose the frequency of interest payouts. Plus, your investment is backed by the security of our bank, giving you peace of mind.

Take advantage of the benefits of fixed deposits and start growing your savings today. Create a fixed deposit account with us and enjoy the convenience and security of a reliable investment option.
